Ingredients

  • 2 scoops of protein powder
  • 10g baking powder
  • 10g low calorie cocoa
  • 1 banana
  • 1 spoon of skyr
  • 50g low calorie butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 spoons (optional) of coconut flour

Way Of Preparation.

In order to make these protein chocolate muffins, mash the banana and melt the butter.

Mix dry ingredients: protein, cocoa, and baking powder.

In another dish, beat the eggs and add the melted butter, after that add skyr.

Add skyr in beaten eggs and after that add mixed dry ingredients.

If the mixture is too much diluted add more coconut.

To get a sweeter mixture you can add honey or peanut butter.

Distribute the mixture into muffin tins.

Bake in a preheated oven at 180Â° for about 20 minutes.
