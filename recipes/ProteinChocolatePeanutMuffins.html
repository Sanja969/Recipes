Protein Chocolate Peanut Muffins

"Lets try something different, it is easier than you expect ㋡."

    Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 2 white eggs
  • 1 scoops of protein powder
  • 70g fine cheese
  • 70g cottage (farmer) cheese
  • 50g peanut butter and optionaly coarsely chopped peanut
  • 10g coconut
  • Stevia or some similar sweetener (honey)
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 10g peanut for decoration

Way Of Preparation.

Beat the eggs and white eggs.

Add rest ingredients except peanut for decorating.

Distribute the mixture into muffin tins (2/3 in every cup) and add peanuts to the top.

Bake in a preheated oven at 200° for about 12-15 minutes.

Cool the muffins after they are baked.
