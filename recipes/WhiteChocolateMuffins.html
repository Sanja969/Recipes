White Chocolate Muffins

"It's hard to resist these beautiful ones 😍."

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 130g sugar
  • 125g Skyr(yogurt)
  • 80ml olive oil
  • 90ml milk
  • 150g flour
  • 10g baking powder
  • 30g cocoa
  • 50g coarsely chopped hazelnuts

For decoration

  • 50g white chocolate
  • chocolate eggs (miny)

Way Of Preparation.

In big dish mix flour, cocoa, baking powder and a little bit of salt.

In another dish, beat the eggs with mixer, than add Skyr, milk and olive oil.

After to beat these ingrediens add dry mixture.

Add hazelnuts and make good homogenized mixture.

Distribute the mixture into muffin tins (2/3 in every cup).

Bake in a preheated oven at 180° for about 12-15 minutes.

Cool the muffins after they are baked.

Melt the white chocolate (on steam).

On every muffin put the same amount of white chocolate and chocolate eggs.
